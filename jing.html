Yanzi Mountain Forest Park


       Yanzishan Forest Park is located in Lingbao City. It was named as Yanzishan National Forest Park in Henan Province on December 27, 2006 with the approval of the State Forestry Administration. The total area is 4776 hectares. The main peak of the park is 1497 meters above sea level, with an average altitude of 1080 meters.
